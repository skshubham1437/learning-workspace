# User wants conceptual depth, not just recipes

The user's motivation is to "be a better developer" rather than to ship a specific project right now. This means lessons should prioritise *why* things work the way they do (e.g., why layers matter in Docker, why CI and CD are separate concerns) over just providing working YAML to copy. Retrieval practice and mental-model questions will be more valuable than complex hands-on exercises at this stage.

## Implications
- Each lesson should include a "why does this matter?" section, not just "how to do it."
- Exercises should test understanding, not just execution.
- It's fine to build small example apps, but the apps are vehicles for learning, not the goal.
