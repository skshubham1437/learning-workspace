# Phase 1: Foundations & Design (~1.5 weeks)

## Modules

### Module 01 — Foundations
What microservices are and why they exist. Conway's Law. Monolith vs Modular Monolith vs Microservices. DDD and Bounded Contexts. Service boundaries. Real case studies (Amazon, Netflix, Uber, Spotify).

### Module 02 — Service Design
Decomposition strategies (business capability, domain, subdomain). Good vs bad service boundaries. The "death by a thousand services" anti-pattern.

## Goal
By the end of this phase, you should be able to look at any monolithic codebase and identify where service boundaries *should* go — and more importantly, where they *shouldn't*.
